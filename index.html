Welcome to the world of Ferrari and Fanta, two sassy senior gals who where rescued from a boring life at the shelter and came to the bustling home of the Guerrero-Pintos.

About the Jewels

Born who knows when and where, Ferrari and Fanta have overcome the sad realities of shelter pups. Two lucky senior girls that found their way to the warm welcoming home of Carla and Oscar have had no trouble adjusting to the warm meals and cozy beds. Needing attention 24/7, Ferrari and Fanta bring their own unique zest to any situation. Planning on eating? Throw them a fry. Thinking of snuggling on the sofa? Bring them in. Will they bark at each other? Yes! Do they hate each other? Most likely. But deep down, they love to be in company of each other. ❤️

Ferrari (aka FerBear)

With a name like Ferrari, you might think she's fast, agile, and has unparalled elegance like the luxury cars. This is not that kind of Ferrari. Chill, lowkey and a lot bitey, Ferrari is the type to like you, maybe hate you but will definitely need you. With thick, lucious fur, petting her is a treat. But our sassy girl has her boundaries and would like for you to know them ✨magically✨.

Fanta

A refreshing addition to any moment, Fanta is a chilled coke! I mean girl, shes a chill girl! She likes to patiently sit on your lap and let you pet her adorable ears. No biting here, no sudden boundaries to watch for. Fanta is a ✨sweet✨ girl who has worked hard to let go of her social anxiety. Bald patch where?!

Health (& Safety)

Meals

With both girls being seniors, their health is of upmost importance. Needing attention 24/7 to exist, Fanta and Ferrari also need to eat at every possible moment. Feeding time is extremely important to these gals, being late by a single moment will result in abusive barking.
Join us at 7am sharp every day for breakfast. And 5pm every day for dinner. A warm scoop of ground turkey and veggies will fill their tummies. Poopies will soon follow.
Ferrari requires her thyroid pills, so lets not forget those in each meal!

Attention

Both girls love to be pet as often as they can. But the reality is that free hands are not always available to pet them. Giving attention to these girls is crucial. Fanta may get anxious if she doesn't see anyone for hours. Her tummy acts up and she may share that lack of attention in unpleasant ways. Please pay attention to your pups, they miss you!

Safety

As with any dog, we proceed with caution. We have to always respect their boundaries and remind ourselves that they have limits too. Ferrari loves to be petted, but has her limits. She might not always know what they are, but when she tells you to stop, you stop! Fanta is a little more shy and might not be as upfront. But she still likes her time to herself. Fanta is a quiet girl that enjoys having the sofa to herself, her bed to herself, and her food to herself. She might have had a rough past and might feel like she has to defend her territory often. For her safety and comfort, we must respect her alone time!
These girls come from homes that could not take care of them anymore. Shelters are no joke, and all we can do is be patient with them. They finally have a safe space to call home, but they still need their ✨space✨. Patience, love and acceptance are all they ask for.
